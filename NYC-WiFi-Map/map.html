This visualization is a jumping off point for the correlation between WiFi and infrastructure with school district performance. Here are the variables:

Hotspots:

  1. Establishment Date: This is an overlay of hotspots in NYC by activation date (sorted by year). Hotspots with unreliable activation data have been minimized.
  2. Provider: This is an overlay of the top three public WiFi providers in NYC. Hotspots serviced by other providers have been minimized.
  3. Free or Limited Free: This is an overlay of all the free and limited free hotspots in NYC.

School Districts

  1. Graffiti: Districts are sorted by the number of reported graffiti incidents.
  2. Noise Complaints: Districts are sorted by the number of reported noise complaints.
  3. Public Assistance: Districts are sorted by the level of people living on public assistance.
  4. School Attendance: Districts are sorted by total percentage of student attendance.
  5. School Enrollment: Districts are sorted by the number of students attending schools in this district.

We hope this data comparison will be an inspiration into further research between the correlations between investing in technology and educational outcomes!
